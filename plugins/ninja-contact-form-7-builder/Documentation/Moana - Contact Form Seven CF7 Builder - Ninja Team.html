INSTALLATION & SETUP

Installation guide:

Follow these steps and see the resolute result within 1 minute.

  1. Download the plugin from CodeCanyon, unpack it and upload the [Moana – Contact Form 7 Builder] folder to your wp-content/plugins/ directory.
  2. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins‘ menu in WordPress.
  3. Go to your main WordPress left menu > Contact to edit your form use WPBakery Page Builder.
  4. Make sure you have already installed WPBakery Page Builder.

Done! Enjoy!
